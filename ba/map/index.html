The Prijedor genocide, refers to numerous war crimes committed during the Bosnian war by the Serb political and military leadership mostly on Bosniak civilians in the Prijedor region of Bosnia-Herzegovina. Around 5,200 Bosniaks and Croats from Prijedor are missing or were killed during the massacre period.
